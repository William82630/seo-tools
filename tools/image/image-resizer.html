How to Use
  1. Drag and drop your image or click "Choose File"
  2. Enter desired width and height
  3. Choose whether to maintain aspect ratio
  4. Click "Resize Image"
  5. Download your resized image
Features
  • Custom width and height settings
  • Option to maintain aspect ratio
  • Instant preview
  • Supports all image formats
  • No upload required - processing happens in your browser
