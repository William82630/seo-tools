How to Use
  1. Upload your image by dragging & dropping or clicking "Choose File"
  2. Choose a preset size or enter custom dimensions
  3. Toggle "Maintain Aspect Ratio" if needed
  4. Click "Resize" to process your image
  5. Click "Download" to save the resized image
Tip: Keep "Maintain Aspect Ratio" checked to prevent image distortion. The tool will automatically calculate the best dimensions.
