Frequently Asked Questions

A SERP Preview shows how your webpage will appear in Google search results. It helps you optimize your meta title and description for better click-through rates by showing exactly how they'll look to users.

Meta titles should be between 50-60 characters to avoid truncation in search results. Meta descriptions should be between 150-160 characters for optimal display. However, Google may display different lengths depending on the device and query.
