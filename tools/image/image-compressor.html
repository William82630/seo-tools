How to Use
  1. Upload your image by dragging & dropping or clicking "Choose File"
  2. Adjust the compression level:
    • Higher values maintain better quality but larger file size
    • Lower values result in smaller files but may reduce quality
  3. Click "Compress" to process your image
  4. Click "Download" to save the compressed image
Tip: Start with a higher compression level and gradually decrease it until you find the optimal balance between file size and quality.
