How to Use
  1. Upload your image by dragging & dropping or clicking "Choose File"
  2. Select the desired output format (PNG, JPEG, WebP, or GIF)
  3. Adjust the quality slider if needed
  4. Click "Convert" to process your image
  5. Click "Download" to save the converted image
Tip: PNG is best for images with transparency, JPEG for photos, WebP for web optimization, and GIF for animations.
