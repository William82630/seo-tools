Frequently Asked Questions

Schema markup is a code (semantic vocabulary) that you put on your website to help search engines return more informative results for users. It helps search engines understand your content better and can lead to rich snippets in search results.

After generating the schema markup, copy the JSON-LD code and paste it into the HTML head section of your webpage. You can also implement it through Google Tag Manager or your content management system's SEO plugins.
