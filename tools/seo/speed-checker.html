Frequently Asked Questions

Page speed is how quickly your website loads and becomes interactive. It's crucial for SEO and user experience, as faster pages rank better in search results and keep users engaged.

Google recommends aiming for under 2 seconds for desktop and under 3 seconds for mobile. However, the faster the better, as even small improvements can significantly impact user experience and conversion rates.
